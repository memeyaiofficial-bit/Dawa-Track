Accessibility Guidelines

Font Sizes for Readability

  • H1 (Page Titles): 32px - Bold
  • H2 (Section Titles): 24px - Bold
  • Body Text: 16px minimum - Regular (Large for elderly patients)
  • Labels: 14px - Regular
  • Small Text: 12px - Regular (Use sparingly)

Color Contrast

  • Text on Background: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA) ✓
  • Large Text (18px+): Minimum 3:1 ratio ✓
  • Interactive Elements: Clearly visible on focus

Touch Targets & Usability

  • Minimum Button Height: 44px (mobile accessibility)
  • Minimum Button Width: 120px
  • Link Underlines: Present for clarity
  • Form Labels: Always visible (not just placeholders)
  • Icons + Text: Used together for clarity

Healthcare-Specific Design

  • Time Format: 24-hour (08:30 AM = 08:30) for medical accuracy
  • Dates: ISO format (2025-02-09) or DD/MM/YYYY (09/02/2025)
  • Medical Terminology: Consistent and clear
  • Confirmation Dialogs: For critical actions (delete, prescribe, discharge)
  • Status Indicators: Icons + text (not color alone)
